Ambigu

Description

Mexican salad made of potato, carrots, peas, chicken and mayonnaise.

Ingredients

  • 4 potato
  • 4 carrots
  • 1 can of peas
  • 2 cooked chicken breast
  • 2 mayonnaise spoon

    • Steps

    1. Wash and prepare all the vegetables to be cooked.
    2. Put 3 liters of water in a pot, put it on the stove and wait it to start boiling.
    3. Add the vegetables and cook it for 8 minutes.
    4. Put the vegetables apart and reserve for later.
    5. shred the 2 cooked chicken breast, Pro tip: use 2 forks to shred the meat.
    6. Put all the Ingredients in a big recipent and add the mayonnaise.
    7. Enjoy it!
