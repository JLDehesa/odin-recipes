Rice

Description

Easy rice recipe to make a delicious garnish to complement almost any plate.

Ingredients

  • 2 rice cups
  • 4 water cups
  • salt to taste

    • Steps

    1. Wash and prepare the rice.
    2. Put the rice on the pot where you'll cook the rice and add 1 table spoon oil.
    3. Cook until the rice change it's color to almost brown.
    4. Add the 4 water cups to the same pot.
    5. Wait for the water to boil, add salt.
    6. Close the pot lid and make the fire to the minimum and cook for 20 minutes.
    7. Enjoy it!
